About

If you've made it this far, I hope this site has been successful in portraying even just a little about who I am and what I'm passionate about! I'm excited to venture into the biotechnology field by pursuing a PhD with a focus on the synthetic biology side of bioengineering/biomedical engineering. I've already worked with some of the top researchers in the world who have trained me and provided me the opportunity to grow as both a person and a researcher, and I'm excited to continue to learn and collaborate with my peers and mentors in the future.​ 

I find great joy in my research and often get lost in it by focusing my efforts on solving unanswered problems. My research started in food science, transitioned to plant pathology, and has now ventured into stem cells. I'm excited to continue on my path as I grow as a person and a researcher with my future work focusing on developing new technologies to attack cancer cells. I'm always interested in learning from new people and progressing my research abilities to help transform and advance the industry. I'm very passionate about sharing my work, whether successful or not, because I find openly providing knowledge to others is crucial to more quickly developing technologies to save lives.  

Since my biggest weakness is getting too absorbed in my work, I recently adapted a hobby that I can do just about anywhere. I recently acquired my first non-iPhone camera (a Nikon D5600) and have had the chance to learn photography with the help of my two best friends, Bailey and Cali. As my girlfriend of over two years, Bailey helps me edit my photos and pick out the good from the bad while enforcing my photography hobby. Bailey's dog Cali (the sweetest golden retriever I have ever met) is my primary model and helps make my photography skills seem better than they actually are. I'm working to also incorporate landscape photographs, portraits, and close-up images but those are still a work in progress...​ 

I'm always looking to connect with new people so don't hesitate to reach out - let's talk! I love talking about research, sports, photography, dogs, and just about anything else so I can't wait to connect!
